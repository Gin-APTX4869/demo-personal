"My teaching program at FSU has been evenly balanced between advanced undergraduate courses and graduate courses. The major focus of my classes up to now has been physical meteorology, data assimilation and mesoscale numerical weather prediction. At the undergraduate level I have taught the required senior course in "Atmospheric Physics I" for the falls of 1998-2002. In the springs of 1998 and 1999, I taught the graduate course in "Atmospheric Data Assimilation". In the spring of 2000, I taught another graduate course in "Numerical Weather Prediction" with emphasis on the mesoscale.
